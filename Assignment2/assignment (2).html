About LIP


It all began as a dream and a plan on a paper. It started with the introduction of the “Real Estate Maximum” concept in 2019 in Cairo, Egypt, by the founder Seif, which revolutionized the real estate market. The idea was simple: attracting the best real estate agents by providing various competitive advantages all under the LIP brand. Better commission plans for associates, a unique business model, an exclusive training system, and state-of-the-art technology helped LIP take off. The idea has since proven highly successful and enabled us to evolve into an extensive international network of real estate professionals.
